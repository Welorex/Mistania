## Perk Tree
The perk tree is structured from the first tier at the top and the next afterwards, the tiers are rookie, pro, phenom, and legend. Your only allowed to chose the next tier if you completed the current tiers base perk (in bold). All abilities only effect Pistols, Precision rifles, and sniper rifles.
