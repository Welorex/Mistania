## Armor
Armor adds to your AC (Armor rating) your role gives you proficiency with certain types of armor, see table:

| Name                | Type   | AR  | Conditions       | Effects                                   |
| ------------------- | ------ | --- | ---------------- | ----------------------------------------- |
| Leathers            | Light  | 1   |                  |                                           |
| Kevlar              | Light  | 2   |                  |                                           |
| Light Armorjack     | Light  | 3   |                  |                                           |
| Bodyweight Suit     | Medium | 4   | 13 Body          | -3 Stealth                                |
| Medium Armorjack    | Medium | 5   | 14 Body          | -3 Stealth                                |
| Heavy Armorjack     | Heavy  | 6   | 15 Body          | DIS Stealth, -10ft Movement               |
| Flak                | Heavy  | 7   | 16 Body          | DIS Stealth -10ft Movement                |
| Bullet proof shield | Shield |     | Takes up one arm | Blocks 15hp of damage, afterwards breaks. |
